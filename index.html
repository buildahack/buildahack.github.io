FAQ

FAQ

>>> What should I bring?
  • Laptop with charger
  • Mobile with charger
  • Optional :
    • Ethernet cable
    • Pendrive
    • Pen and paper
>>> What if I don’t have a team or an idea?
If you don’t have a team now, we'll help you out building a team on the spot!
>>> How many people per team?
Maximum 3 students per team and they don’t have to be from the same college as yours. You may also hack solo if you wish.
>>> Should I bring extra hardware?
Yes, you may bring additional hardware if you have a project in mind that requires it.
>>> What should I prepare prior to the event?
It’s a good idea to setup your computer with some development software like:
  • Your favourite IDE
  • A version control tool (git)
  • Any other softwares/Libraries/APIs that you may need
>>> Should I start my project before the hackathon?
No. All work on projects should be done at the event. You can read up on different APIs and frameworks but all code should be written during Build-a-hack.
>>> How will my project be judged?
Industry experts will browse the demos at the end of the event, asking questions and choosing their favorites. From these selections, the best-of-the-best will be selected as winners.
>>> What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?
Reach out to us at contact@buildahack.com for any other queries !
>>> What if I'm new to programming?
That's totally ok! Hackathons are a great place to learn. We're going to have tutorials and mentors to support you during the event. Their would also be 2-3 pre-sessions to help you get started.
>>> What do I get by attending Build-A-Hack?
Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker community to internships offers from some cool startups. And yeah awesome prizes(worth more than 6 lakhs) too!
>>> What are the themes?
  • Education
  • Internet
  • Health
  • Internet of things (IOT)
  • Open source
  • Open track
    • >>> Who can participate?
    Any college or university students are welcome to participate! If you are passionate about programming and are interested in new technologies, we invite you to join Build-A-Hack!
    >>> How much will it cost?
    Absolutely nothing! :D
    >>> Will I need to pay for food?
    Nope! Food is on us.
